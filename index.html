01
The problem

I just needed
a flat.
Here's what
I found instead.

You'd think in 2026 there would be a simple way to get a clean technical drawing of a t-shirt. There isn't.

You Google "fashion flat template." You scroll through dozens of results — different proportions, different styles, different quality. Nothing matches what you have in your head. You find something close, maybe on a marketplace. You pay for it. You open the file: ungrouped layers, weird anchor points, no logical structure.

You try to edit it. Change the neckline, adjust the sleeves. Thirty minutes later you're still fighting with the pen tool. And none of it is made for production.

Or you find a tool that actually works. Real professional software with accurate measurements and proper construction. That'll be €5,000. Maybe more.

Spend hours on repetitive manual work, or spend thousands on something that should be accessible to anyone who works with garments. There's something broken about that.
